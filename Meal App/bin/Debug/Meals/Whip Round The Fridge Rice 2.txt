Boil a pan of water and add the rice.
Cook for 8-10 mins till cooked but still firm.
Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan.
Fry the bacon pieces till crisp and tip onto a plate.
Add the onion and garlic and fry till soft.
Drain the rice then add to the pan.
Add the crispy bacon, butter, frozen peas and chicken.
Heat through, stirring all the time till piping hot.
Stir in the chopped tomatoes, soy sauce and seasoning.
Give everything a good stir and serve.
