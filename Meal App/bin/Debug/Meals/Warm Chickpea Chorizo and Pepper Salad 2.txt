
Cut the chorizo into 3cm thick slices.

Heat the oil in a large frying pan over a high heat.

Cook the chorizo for 4-5 mins.

Turn chorizo occasionally till the edges start to crisp and its oil has been released.


Tip the chickpeas into the pan.

Cook for 2 mins until hot.

Add the peppers.

Cook for a few minutes till everything is juicy.

Remove from the heat.

Stir in the coriander.
Ladle into two bowls and top each with a spoonful of yogurt.
