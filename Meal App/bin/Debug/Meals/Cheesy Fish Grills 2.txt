

Preheat the grill to High.

Lightly oil a large shallow heatproof dish.

Arrange the fillet in the dish.

Brush them with a little oil.

Grill for 2 mins.


Remove the dish from the grill.

Turn the fish over.

Top each fillet with a scrunched slice of ham.

Mix together the cheese and onions and scatter them over the fish.

Season with salt and pepper.

Return to the grill for 5 mins until the fish flakes easily.


Serve with green vegetables such as broccoli or stir-fried cabbage.
